Fufu And Egusi Soup

List Of Ingredients

  • fufu
  • melon (egusi)
  • palm oil
  • pepper
  • maggi
  • salt
  • meat
  • crafish
  • stock fish (okporokpo)
  • vegetable

Steps:

  1. put the pot on fire
  2. Per-boil the rice with a little quantity of salt
  3. add the palm oil and make a pepper sauce
  4. add the stock fish already washed and heated, crafish and meat
  5. then add the melon already mixed with water and some people add raw egg but optional
  6. allow the egusi to heat
  7. then add the sliced vegetable already washed and heated with hot water
  8. cover the pot and allow to heat
  9. taste and add maggi or salt if needed
  10. Serve with the pounded fufu
  11. food is ready

Fried egg and bread

List Of Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • groundnut Oil
  • Tomatoes and Pepper
  • salt
  • maggit

Steps:

  1. Slice the tomatoes and pepper
  2. Break the eggs into a small bowl
  3. Add salt and maggi to the egg
  4. Pour the groundnut oil into a frying pan
  5. Fry the tomatoes and pepper for 5 minutes
  6. Add the mixed egg to the frying tomatoes and pepper
  7. Fry more for few mintes
  8. Sandwich the egg in the bread or eat the bread with the egg sauce
  9. The food is ready
